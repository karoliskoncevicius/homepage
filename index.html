Karolis Koncevičius

Chronoepigenetics Laboratory, Institute of Biotechnology, Life Sciences Center, Vilnius, Lithuania.

karolis.koncevicius@gmail.com

About

My work combines biomedical research, applied statistics, computational biology, and R programming.

Currently focusing on epigenome-based prediction of chronological and biological age.
